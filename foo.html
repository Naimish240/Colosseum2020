All work no play makes Jack a dull boy

Thus came Colosseum into existence. If academics are the daily bread and butter of college students life, then sports fests are the Saturday night parties we all crave. At no other time will the students of an institution be seen displaying so much enthusiasm, receptiveness and innovation. SASTRA proudly entered the tradition of holding annual Sports Fests with Colosseum in 2008. Hosting 9 games with more than 1500 participants from different geographies of the country, over four days of complete gaming and sports, Colosseum has carved a niche for itself in the world of Sports Fests. Bringing to you the most awaited Sport Event of the season.

Rules and Security measures :
  • Only B.Tech/M.Tech/5 year M.Tech Integrated/Law Students who are born on or after 01 / 07 / 1996 are eligible for participation
  • Only participants and team managers will be allowed inside the campus
  • Only 2 team managers are allowed (faculties with institutional ID card).
  • Only college vehicles are allowed inside the campus
  • Team member should have the bonafide id card in provided format along with their Institutional ID cards. ID cards should be complete including 4 signatures and 4 seals
  • The ID card will be considered invalid if not complete.
  • Decision of tournament committee on all matters relating to the tournament shall be final and binding
  • Any team arguing with the referee will be viewed strictly and can be disqualified
  • Indisciplinary behaviour by any members will lead to direct disqualification of the respective institution
  • All team should report 15 min before the start of the match and teams late by more than half an hour will lose by giving opponent a walk over
  • Male participants are not allowed in to the women's accommodation and vice-versa
  • The organisers will not be responsible for any loss or theft of personal belongings
  • Only the college vehicles of the participating teams will be permitted within the campus
  • Consumption of alcohol and smoking is strictly prohibited in the campus premises
  • All participants must bring a passport size photograph
